How your donation makes a difference

It is your donation that enables us to actively keep campaigning for animals in need and improving animal welfare standards worldwide. Specifically, you can see how your donation contributes towards the betterment and welfare of animals below.

Your donation will go to Best Friends Save Animal Society.
All, supporting crucial animal rescue efforts to give every pet a second chance.

  • When you donate €25
    You can provide food, toys, and treats for rescued animals to help them feel safe and loved.
  • When you donate €50
    You can provide vaccines for up to five Community Cats - feral, stray, and friendly cats living outdoors.
  • When you donate €100
    You can provide a shelter dog with special enrichment while they wait for their new home.
